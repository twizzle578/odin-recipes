Kimbap

Description

Ingredients

  • Nori
  • White Rice
  • Furikake
  • Mayo
  • Cucumber
  • Korean Pickled Radish
  • Avocado
  • Korean Fish Cake
  • Spinach
  • Carrot

Steps

  • Place a sheet of nori on your bamboo rolling mat.
  • Spread rice over 1/3 of the sheet of nori.
  • Place your other ingredients on the rice.
  • Roll your kimbap using the bamboo rolling mat.
